# Mission

Build an AI-powered investment research and portfolio construction platform that continuously discovers, evaluates, ranks and monitors India's highest-quality companies.

AlphaForge is not a stock screener.

AlphaForge is an intelligent portfolio construction engine.

---

# Core Objective

Automatically identify the best long-term investment opportunities from the entire NSE universe and maintain an optimized portfolio of only the highest conviction companies.

---

# Investment Philosophy

Quality over Quantity.

Instead of owning hundreds of companies, AlphaForge will identify a concentrated portfolio of only the strongest businesses.

Target Portfolio Size:

12 Stocks

Maximum Watchlist:

30 Stocks

Universe:

Entire NSE Listed Companies
