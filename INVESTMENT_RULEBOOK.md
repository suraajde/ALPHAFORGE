# Purpose

This document defines the investment philosophy and decision-making rules of AlphaForge.

Every recommendation generated by AlphaForge must follow these rules.

If a future feature conflicts with this rulebook, the rulebook takes precedence.

# Replacement Rules

Replacement should never occur due to price movement alone.

Replacement should occur only when:

Fundamentals deteriorate.

Quality deteriorates.

Business outlook changes materially.

A stronger company enters the Top 12.

The Alpha Score declines significantly.
